## Role For This Project

This page is a reference for the Sasha Belokonova landing prototype, not a copy
source. Use it to understand persuasive structure, section rhythm, offer
packaging, and mobile/desktop adaptations. Keep copy, brand, imagery, and coach
identity original for the Sasha Belokonova app.

## Positioning Pattern

- Product: summer fitness method with home workouts, weekly nutrition guidance,
  and expert add-ons.
- Main promise: regain lightness, energy, and body shape during summer without
  gym friction or restrictive dieting.
- Main proof: large participation count, before/after collage, message
  screenshots, coach credentials, and multiple guarantee/support claims.
- Main objection handling: contrasts against dieting, gym complexity, random
  free videos, payment problems, equipment worries, age/level concerns, and
  access issues after payment.
- Main conversion path: repeated pink CTA buttons scroll to the tariff/payment
  block with three offers.

## Visual And UX Pattern

- Palette is mostly white and very pale pink with hot-pink accent buttons,
  labels, dividers, and highlighted text.
- Layout is a long single-column conversion page. Desktop uses centered content
  with wider horizontal card rows; mobile stacks almost everything into narrow
  cards.
- Hero uses a large cutout athletic woman on the right/upper area and a strong
  text block on the left/under it depending on viewport.
- Sections alternate between white and pale-pink bands. Cards are white with
  soft shadows, rounded corners, compact text, small pink icons, and occasional
  pill labels.
- The page duplicates many sections in HTML for desktop/mobile variants.
  Expect repeated text nodes when extracting content.
- A ticker-style strip repeats the social-proof message across the page width.
- Several result/coach blocks use horizontal mobile carousels with "swipe
  right" prompts.
- FAQ answers are present in the DOM and displayed through accordion rows.

## Section Order

1. Hero with summer method promise, primary CTA, VPN notice, countdown, and
   discount urgency.
2. Social-proof ticker about more than 590,000 women.
3. Benefits block: energy, lightness/body shape, confidence, plus exercise
   visual.
4. Results block: before/after collage and testimonial screenshots.
5. Program contents block: method workouts, nutrition lectures, nutrition
   course, breathing/posture training, fat-burning course.
6. Tariff/payment band with countdown and three packages.
7. Payment help block with Telegram, VK, email, and MAX contacts.
8. Why the method works versus diets, gyms, and random free videos.
9. Coach credibility block with trainer experience, motherhood result, titles,
   and quote.
10. Final urgency CTA band with 82% discount.
11. Guarantee/assurance block: price, uniqueness, result, support, convenience.
12. FAQ accordion.
13. Legal footer and social/contact icons.
14. Hidden/embedded GetCourse order forms for the three tariffs.

## Offers

- Light Start: 3,140 RUB discounted from 6,400 RUB, 51% off, 2 months access.
  Includes the main method and 5 nutrition lectures.
- Transformation: 5,490 RUB discounted from 22,380 RUB, 75% off, 3 months
  access. Marked as the majority choice. Adds the nutrition course and
  breathing/posture training.
- Maximum: 6,890 RUB discounted from 37,280 RUB, 82% off, 6 months access.
  Marked for maximum result. Adds the fat-burning course.

## Interaction And Links

- CTA buttons route toward `#form` / tariff payment area.
- A "Подробнее" click can open a compact detail view for a category/program.
  The observed view is titled "Тренировки дома" and shows one large bordered
  product card: coach cutout image on the left, pink "ФЛАГМАН" pill, "Метод
  Усмановой" title, short explanatory copy, and a pink rounded "Подробнее"
  button. The composition is centered on a light-gray page background with a
  hot-pink rounded border around the card.
- A second observed card in the same "Тренировки дома" detail view uses the
  same horizontal image/text/button template but without the flagship border.
  It is labeled "Марафон", titled "Стройности", and describes a visible
  21-day result for users starting from zero. This suggests the "Подробнее"
  experience is a category page or category section listing multiple programs,
  with one highlighted flagship item followed by regular program cards.
- Payment-support links: Telegram bot, VK community, email
  `help@usmanovasport.ru`, and MAX bot.
- Legal links point to offer, privacy policy, and personal-data consent pages
  on the same domain.
- Cookie banner appears at the bottom with an OK action.
- Forms are GetCourse order forms with hidden product-specific fields and
  "continue" actions for each tariff.

## Adaptation Notes For Sasha Belokonova App

- Preserve the useful conversion arc: promise, proof, contents, tariffs,
  objection handling, coach credibility, guarantee, FAQ.
- Avoid copying page copy or Katya Usmanova-specific credentials.
- If borrowing structure, create original Sasha-specific proof, program names,
  visuals, and prices.
- For the current prototype, the most relevant patterns are the pale-pink
  sectional rhythm, repeated CTAs, compact program cards, three-tier offer
  comparison, and FAQ/payment-support closure.
- The Sasha "Подробнее" template should use the full reference arc even when
  the observed original detail view is compact: hero, audience fit, contents,
  outcomes, offer tiers, objections, coach trust, assurances, FAQ, and final
  CTA. This makes each program-specific hash page a real derivative landing
  page instead of a short card expansion.
